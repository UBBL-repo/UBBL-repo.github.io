We thrive to conduct impactful research that we are excited and proud of it. Our lab performs basic and applied research on ultrasonics, dynamics and control, and cellular biomechanics and mechanobiology to support the discovery and translation to the clinics of novel diagnostic and therapeutic interventions against human disease. Specific areas of our research include cell-based diagnostics and therapeutics, microbubble dynamics modeling and control, linear and nonlinear wave propagation in complex media (skull), image guided therapy, and (brain) cancer research.
