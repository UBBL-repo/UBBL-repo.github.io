We engineer ultrasound instrumentation tailored for precise brain applications. Our team builds acoustic holograms, phased arrays, and control electronics to shape pressure fields through the skull.

Using a combination of 3D printing, hydrophone mapping, and simulation tools, we iteratively optimize transducers and control circuits to support focused ultrasound therapy and imaging.

These tools are used in conjunction with machine learning-based controllers and real-time feedback mechanisms to deliver personalized and adaptive treatments.
